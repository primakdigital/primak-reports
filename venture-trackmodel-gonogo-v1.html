7 Market Gaps — What Nobody Is Doing
1. No physical + digital layer (AR/telemetry) on any track model product
2. No topographic/elevation-accurate models (Spa, Suzuka, COTA have famous elevation — all rendered flat today)
3. No personalization — "your lap, your data, your date" doesn't exist
4. MotoGP / motorcycle track day = underserved (market is F1-fan focused, not rider-focused)
5. Track day participants (riders) barely served — entire market targets spectators/fans
6. Mid-market $100-$300 premium gift category is empty (below = commoditized Etsy PLA, above = signed memorabilia)
7. No subscription/collection model ("circuit of the month")

AR/VR — Verdict
AR is NOT a gimmick for this product — because the data is personal (rider's GPS, speed, lean angle). But it's NOT the MVP.

MVP (Phase 1): QR code → web page with 3D track + rider's telemetry overlay. Cost: $6-14K. Uses Google model-viewer (free) + Three.js.

Phase 2: Camera AR overlay on physical model. +$9-17K.

Phase 3: Dedicated app with direct RaceChrono/Harry's LapTimer import. $47-120K.

Recommendation: Launch physical-only. Add QR→web data layer once product-market fit is proven. Camera AR is Phase 3, not Phase 2.
Roadmap

Phase 1 — Validate (Month 1-3)

FDM printer ($600) + 3-5 popular tracks (Mugello, Spa, Barcelona, Silverstone, Suzuka).
Sell on Etsy at $79-$99. Target: 20-30 units/month.
Test personalization (name + date + lap time engraving).
Investment: ~$1,500. Revenue target: $2K-3K/month.

Phase 2 — Optimize (Month 4-8)

Add CNC wood premium line ($149-$199). Test laser-cut layered option.
Own Shopify store. Premium packaging.
Add QR→web data layer (3D track + telemetry). Build: $6-14K.
Target: 50-80 units/month. Revenue: $5-8K/month.

Phase 3 — Scale (Month 9+)

Injection mold top sellers ($5-10K/mold). Two tiers: accessible ($69) + premium ($149-$249).
Camera AR. Subscription drops. B2B to track day organizers.
Fulfillment service (ShipBob). Target: 100-200 units/month.
Risks
HIGH Physical product = not passive. Production, shipping, QC, returns. This is e-commerce, not SaaS.
MED 62-95 units/month for $5K profit. Achievable but requires marketing + community work.
MED Free STL files online = price pressure on basic models. Differentiation must come from finish, materials, personalization.
LOW Motorcycle niche specifically unvalidated (Golf Project validates the model, not the audience).
LOW AR development cost ($6-14K for web layer). Manageable, and deferred to Phase 2.
