03 - Voice & Tone

How we say it

Primary Language
English-first
LinkedIn, website, proposals, published content = English. Israeli DMs = Hebrew. Match client language in proposals.
Voice Character
Chill authority
Sounds like someone who already has, not someone who's proving. Direct. Confident. Numbers > adjectives.

Do

  • "We" - company language, not freelancer
  • Numbers first, always. "$585M" not "significant exit"
  • Short sentences. Let facts breathe.
  • Lay credentials, let target connect dots
  • Chill test: does this sound like someone who already has?
  • "Built the product" / "Shipped" / "Deployed"
  • Match the register of who you're talking to

Don't

  • Never first person in pitches ("I am passionate...")
  • Never "Passionate about..." / "Thought leader"
  • Never buzzwords without receipts
  • Never diagnose their problems in outreach
  • Never adopt opponent's diminishing framing
  • Never over-explain. If it needs a paragraph, it's wrong.
  • Never "Founder" in title. CPO only.
  • Never use "fractional" as identity - only as SEO term in LinkedIn About

Outreach Rules

Intel Protocol (Before ANY outreach)

(1) OSINT on target - find THEIR words for the problem. (2) Map what their org provides vs doesn't. (3) Write using THEIR language, not ours. Lay credentials, let them connect the dots. NEVER diagnose their problems.

How it looks

Established by the CV cornerstone deliverable (23.02.2026). All future Primak outputs inherit this visual system.

#1a1a1a
#555

#f7f7f7
#fff

Black and white only. No accent colors. Grayscale hierarchy creates authority. Photography: B&W, grayscale(100%).

Layout Principles

Dark Hero

Major documents open with dark (#111) hero section, min-height 80vh. Name + title + value proposition + contact.

Card System

Content in bordered cards (1px solid #e0e0e0). Hover: translateY(-2px) + box-shadow. Tier 1 = full cards. Tier 2 = compact grid.

Generous Whitespace

Section padding: 48px 60px. Let content breathe. No cramming. 1 topic per page, max 1 scroll.

Dark Bookends

Open dark, close dark. Education/advisory sections use dark background for visual weight.

Design Reference

Moog - precision + beauty. Not creative portfolio. Executive editorial meets Monocle. Zero icons. Zero decorative elements. The content IS the design.
