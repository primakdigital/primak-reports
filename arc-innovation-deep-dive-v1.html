What Is ARC Innovation?

ARC = Accelerate, Redesign, Collaborate. Founded in 2019 by Prof. Eyal Zimlichman at Sheba Medical Center (Israel's largest hospital, top 10 globally). It's the innovation arm of Sheba -- not just an accelerator but an entire ecosystem.

What ARC Provides to Startups:

  • Clinical validation + regulatory guidance
  • Access to Sheba's physician network for mentorship
  • Direct access to de-identified patient data (25 years of imaging, labs, genetics)
  • Pilot sites inside the hospital
  • Office space (up to 1 year)
  • Capital ($250K via embARC: $125K SAFE 7% + $125K MFN convertible)
  • Global market access via ARC centers worldwide

What ARC Does NOT Provide (The Gap):

NO In-House UX/Product Design Capability

  • ARC provides clinical, regulatory, data, and business mentorship
  • No mention of any design studio, UX team, or product design services anywhere in ARC's ecosystem
  • No design/UX agency listed as a partner
  • Startups are left to find their own UX/product design resources
  • 120+ startups, most building digital health products -- all need UX
  • Early-stage startups (embARC cohort) have $250K -- tight budget, need efficient design partner
