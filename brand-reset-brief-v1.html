החלטה #6 VOICE & LANGUAGE

השאלה
איזו שפה ו-tone Primak מדבר? (ב-LinkedIn, proposals, emails)
PRIMO RECOMMENDATION

English-first + direct + numbers — "LeCun, not TED Talk"

DO
• LinkedIn = English (global positioning)
• Claims backed by numbers
• Short. Max 3 sentences per point
• "I designed X. It did Y. Here's Z."
• Hebrew for Israeli outreach DMs
DON'T
• "Passionate about..."
• "Helping companies with..."
• Buzzwords without proof
• Long storytelling posts
• First person ("I am amazing at...")
